Desert Thorn Keeper is a calm, offline cactus care puzzle. Tend 50 thorny friends across five cactus families, read each symptom, and choose the matching care action to guide every plant from sprout to bloom.

FIVE CACTUS FAMILIES
Each level presents a potted cactus from one of five families — Barrel Cactus, Prickly Pear, Moon Cactus, Saguaro Sprout, or Holiday Cactus. Every family has its own optimal ranges for water, sunlight, and wind, so the correct care action changes depending on which cactus you are tending.

SIX CARE ACTIONS
Tap Water +5, Water +15, or Water +25 to adjust moisture. Use Fertilizer to boost nutrients. Use Drain Soil to remove excess water. Use Pest Care to reduce pest pressure. Weather values like sun and wind drift on their own and cannot be controlled directly — you can only manage water, nutrients, drainage, and pests.

TEN SYMPTOM EVENTS
Symptoms appear during the Growth Prep stage and beyond. Heat Wrinkle, Deep Wilt, Crusted Soil, Wet Foot, Pale Crown, Bud Stall, Scar Specks, Soft Base, Sun Blotch, and Wind Scuff — each one points to a single correct care action that depends on the cactus family. A wrong action during an event ends the level.

TUTORIAL LEVELS
Levels 1 to 3 are tutorial levels. They display the correct care hint during each symptom event so you can learn the patterns. From level 4 onward, symptom events only describe the visible signs, and you must infer the matching care action yourself.

FIFTY LEVELS
The game spans 50 levels with a smooth difficulty curve. Level time limits grow from 60 seconds to 180 seconds. Symptom events become more frequent and shorter as you progress. Progress is saved automatically on your device.

CALM AND OFFLINE
No timers panic, no leaderboards, no stress. Desert Thorn Keeper is fully offline with no ads, no in-app purchases, no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking. Everything stays on your device.

Gameplay Fact Card Summary

Gameplay Fact Card

Core screen: A potted cactus centered on screen with HUD bars at top and six care buttons at bottom.

Controls: Tap one of six care buttons (Water +5/+15/+25, Fertilizer, Drain, Pest) during or outside symptom events.

Goal: Reach 100% growth progress with health above zero to bloom the cactus.

Fail: Health reaches zero (event timeout, wrong care action during event, or prolonged unsafe conditions).

Progression: 50 levels, tutorial 1-3 with hints, 5 cactus families cycle by (level-1) % 5, progress saved locally.
